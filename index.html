Understanding Prediction Markets

Shares as Probability

Each share represents a probability percentage. A share trading at ₹65 implies a 65% chance the event will occur. Buy low if you think the probability is higher, sell high if you think it's lower.

Dynamic Odds

Prices fluctuate based on new information and trading activity. As more traders express their views through buying and selling, the market aggregates collective intelligence into real-time odds.

Settlement

When the event concludes, shares settle at either ₹100 (if the event occurred) or ₹0 (if it didn't). This binary outcome ensures clear resolution and immediate profit/loss calculation.

Profit & Loss

Your profit equals (Settlement Price - Purchase Price) × Number of Shares. Buy at ₹30, event happens, settle at ₹100 = ₹70 profit per share. Simple, transparent, immediate.
